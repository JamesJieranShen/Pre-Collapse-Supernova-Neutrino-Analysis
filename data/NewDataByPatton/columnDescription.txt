These files contain the data for all of the times, separated by empty lines.  These is the volume integrated differential rates and emissivities.

Column descriptions:


Beta File

column 1 - profile number

column 2 - energy (MeV)

column 3 - nuELumNum = number luminosity of nu_e (1/MeV/s)

column 4 - nuEBarLumNum = number luminosity of nu_e_bar (1/MeV/s)

column 5 - nuELumEnergy = energy luminosity of nu_e (1/s)

column 6 - nuEBarLumEnergy = energy luminosity of nu_e_bar (1/s)


All thermal files

column 1 - profile number

column 2 - energy (MeV)

column 3 - nuELumNum = number luminosity of nu_e (1/s)

column 4 - nuEBarLumNum = number luminosity of nu_e_bar (1/s)

column 5 - nuMuLumNum = number luminosity of nu_mu/nu_tau (1/s) (total of nu_x = 2*this column)

column 6 - nuMuBarLumNum = number luminosity of nu_x_bar (1/s)

column 7 - nuELumEnergy = energy luminosity of nu_e (1/MeV/s)

column 8 - nuEBarLumEnergy = energy luminosity of nu_e_bar (1/MeV/s)

column 9 - nuELumEnergy = energy luminosity of nu_x (1/MeV/s)

column 10 - nuEBarLumEnergy = energy luminosity of nu_x_bar (1/MeV/s)
